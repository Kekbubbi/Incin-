Incineroar

Incineroar is a bipedal feline pokemon. He is a Fire/Dark type.

Incineroar!

As you can see, Incineroar is a black and red feline pokemon. They have black stripes, and a flame belt. Massive claws, and a long chest. The black on an Incineroar's face is supposed to reference a mask.
